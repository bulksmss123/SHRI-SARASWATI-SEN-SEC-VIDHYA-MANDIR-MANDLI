About Our School

Shri Saraswati Senior Secondary Vidya Mandir, Mandli (Balotra) is a renowned educational institution committed to holistic development, blending modern pedagogy with traditional values. Our campus in Kalyanpur, Rajasthan fosters academic excellence, sports, arts and moral education.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, a vast library, and dedicated faculty, we empower students to excel in board examinations and life skills. We believe in "Vidya Dadati Vinayam" — knowledge brings humility.
